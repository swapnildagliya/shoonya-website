Membership

Who is a Shoonya member?

A Shoonya member is anyone currently registered in one or more regular weekly dance classes during a semester at Shoonya Dance Centre in Ghent.

Membership is valid for the duration of the semester: September–February or February–June.

Exclusive perks

Being a Shoonya member comes with advantages: enjoy special discounts on festivals, workshops, and other events organised at Shoonya Dance Centre. The more you dance, the more you unlock.

Shoonya ID ≠ Membership

Having a Shoonya ID (a 4-digit number) doesn't automatically grant membership. Membership is tied to active enrollment in regular weekly dance classes within a semester.
