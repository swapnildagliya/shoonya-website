About

What is Bollyfolk?

Each semester we go deep into one Indian folk tradition — the footwork, the rhythm, and the story behind it. Folk dance in India is rooted in celebration: harvests, weddings, festivals, seasons. Every edition is different. No prior experience needed for the Open level; Level 2 builds on the previous semester. You don't need to know anything coming in — just show up.
