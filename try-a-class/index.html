Section 2 — What to expect (paste AFTER the events calendar)

What to expect

Good to know before you come

Registration

None needed. Open Door Days and the trial week are walk-in. No booking, no Shoonya ID required. Check the schedule, show up 10 minutes early, and join in.

What to wear

Comfortable clothing you can move and sweat in. Bring clean indoor shoes — street shoes aren't allowed on the dance floor. Socks are fine for a first visit.

Partner dances

For Cuban Salsa, Bachata, Tango, Kizomba, and Rueda we recommend bringing a partner. Solo visitors are welcome on Open Door Days — we rotate partners during trial classes.

After the trial

Ready to continue? Create a Shoonya ID and register for the semester. Join after week 1 and your price is prorated automatically — you only pay for the remaining sessions.
