What to expect

Good to know before you come

Registration

None needed. Open Door Days and the trial week are walk-in. No booking, no Shoonya ID required. Check the schedule, show up 10 minutes early, and join in.

What to wear

Comfortable clothing you can move and sweat in. Bring clean indoor shoes — street shoes aren't allowed on the dance floor. Socks are fine for a first visit.

Partner dances

For Cuban Salsa, Bachata, Tango, Kizomba, and Rueda we recommend bringing a partner. Solo visitors are welcome on Open Door Days.

After the trial

Ready to continue? Create a Shoonya ID and register for the semester. Registration stays open for 3 weeks — same semester fee, and you'll catch up faster than you think.
